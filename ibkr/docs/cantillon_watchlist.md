# Cantillon Effect Investment Watchlist
*Updated March 12, 2026*

## Thesis
Stock market opportunities come from sectors/companies that receive "new printed money" first (Cantillon Effect). Current money spigots: Fed reserve management purchases (~$40B/mo), massive fiscal deficits ($1.9T projected FY2026), and AI capex (~$600-690B from hyperscalers in 2026).

## Risks & Notes
- AI capex nearly doubled YoY — sustainability is the key question
- Transformer supply deficit (30%) creating real bottlenecks and pricing power
- Defense names have near-zero beta (0.01–0.41) — uncorrelated to tech
- Some second-order names (Vertiv, Comfort Systems, GE Vernova) already re-rated on earnings
- EMCOR beat earnings but market shrugged — possible value opportunity
- POWL has 16.8% short interest despite zero debt — investigate the bear case
- KTOS is speculative (684x trailing P/E, negative FCF)
- **Not financial advice** – do your own due diligence
